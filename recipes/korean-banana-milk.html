Korean Banana Milk

Description

The drink is made with high-quality milk sourced from Korean dairy farms and is blended with real banana puree, giving it a rich and fruity flavor. Binggrae Banana Milk is not only tasty but also nutritious, as it is a good source of calcium, protein, and other essential vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4-5 ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine banana, milk, water, maple syrup and vanilla in the blender cup. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place ice cubes in glass (if using). Pour banana milk on top.
